## 🎯 Logo Concept

### Design Philosophy

The Navet logo represents **"the hub"** (nav in Swedish):

```
        ○
       /|\
      / | \
     /  ●  \      ● = Navet (the hub)
    ○   |   ○     ○ = Connected devices
     \  |  /      ─ = Communication paths
      \ | /
       \|/
        ○
```

**Visual Elements:**
- **Center node** → Your smart home (Navet as the central hub)
- **8 radiating connections** → Your connected devices and systems
- **Network pattern** → Interconnected, unified control
- **Orange gradient** → Warm, welcoming, energetic

**Colors:**
- Primary: `#f97316` (Tailwind orange-500)
- Secondary: `#ea580c` (Tailwind orange-600)
- Elements: White with 95% opacity

## 🎨 Design Guidelines Summary

### ✅ Do's
- Use on clean, neutral backgrounds
- Maintain aspect ratio
- Keep minimum clear space (10%)
- Use SVG format when possible
- Choose appropriate variation for context

### ❌ Don'ts
- Change colors or gradient
- Rotate or distort
- Add effects, shadows, or outlines
- Use on busy backgrounds
- Stretch or squash
